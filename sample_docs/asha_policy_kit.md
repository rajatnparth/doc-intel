# Northwind Motor Insurance — Private Car Policy Kit (2026)

## 1. Policy and Period of Insurance

This Private Car Package Policy is issued by Northwind Motor Insurance ("the
Company") to Asha Rao ("the Insured") under policy number MTR-2026-1147, for the
vehicle described in the Schedule. The period of insurance is twelve (12) months
from the renewal date. The Insured may cancel at any time on seven (7) days
written notice; the Company sends a renewal notice thirty (30) days before
expiry.

## 2. Premium and Payment

The renewal premium is due within fifteen (15) days of the renewal notice.
Instalment premiums carry no grace: cover is suspended while any instalment is
overdue, and claims arising during a period of suspension are not payable.
Premiums are exclusive of applicable taxes.

## 3. Cover and Benefits

The Company covers loss or damage to the insured vehicle (own damage) and legal
liability to third parties as required by law. Windscreen and window glass
replacement is covered without affecting the no-claim status, up to two claims
per period of insurance. A compulsory excess of ₹2,000 applies to each own
damage claim.

## 4. Claims Process

Accidents must be reported to the Company within twenty-four (24) hours. A
surveyor is appointed within forty-eight (48) hours of intimation for own damage
claims. A claim is deemed settled when the Company issues a discharge voucher
and the repairer confirms release of the vehicle. Unresolved claims escalate to
the grievance officer after ten (10) days.

## 5. Damage Assessment Codes

The following codes are used by surveyors and network garages. The Insured
should quote the code when contacting the claims line.

| Damage Code | Severity | Meaning                          | Remedy                                    |
|-------------|----------|----------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| D-4470      | 2        | Cosmetic panel damage            | Book any network garage; no surveyor      |
| D-4471      | 3        | Structural damage suspected      | Hold repairs until the surveyor inspects  |
| D-4472      | 1        | Vehicle not driveable            | Call the roadside line; do not drive      |
| D-4473      | 2        | Third party property involved    | File a police intimation before claiming  |

## 6. Personal Data

The Company processes the Insured's personal data to administer the policy and
settle claims, and shares it with network garages and surveyors only as needed
for a claim. The Insured may request a copy of the records held about them.

## 7. Limit of Liability

The Company's aggregate liability in respect of own damage in any one period of
insurance shall not exceed the Insured's Declared Value of ₹6,45,000 recorded in
the Schedule. The Company shall not be liable for consequential loss,
depreciation, wear and tear, or loss of use, howsoever arising.
