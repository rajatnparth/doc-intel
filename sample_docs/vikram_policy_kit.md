# Northwind Motor Insurance — Private Car Policy Kit (2026)

## 1. Policy and Period of Insurance

This Private Car Package Policy is issued by Northwind Motor Insurance ("the
Company") to Vikram Mehta ("the Insured") under policy number MTR-2026-2210. The
period of insurance is twelve (12) months from the renewal date. The Company
sends a renewal notice twenty (20) days before expiry.

## 2. Premium and Payment

The renewal premium is due within ten (10) days of the renewal notice. Premiums
are exclusive of applicable taxes. The policy lapses if the renewal premium is
not received before the expiry date.

## 3. Cover and Benefits

The Company covers loss or damage to the insured vehicle (own damage) and legal
liability to third parties as required by law. Windscreen and window glass
replacement is covered up to one claim per period of insurance. A compulsory
excess of ₹5,000 applies to each own damage claim. The Insured's Declared Value
is ₹11,20,000 as recorded in the Schedule.

## 4. Claims Process

Accidents must be reported to the Company within forty-eight (48) hours. Own
damage claims at non-network garages are settled on a reimbursement basis
against itemised repair bills.
