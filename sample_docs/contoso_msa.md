# Contoso Master Services Agreement (2024)

## 1. Parties and Term

This Master Services Agreement is entered into between Contoso Manufacturing
("Provider") and the Customer. The initial term is twenty-four (24) months from
the Effective Date.

## 2. Payment Terms

Payment is due within forty-five (45) days of receipt of a valid invoice. Late
amounts accrue interest at 2.0% per month. All fees are exclusive of applicable
taxes. Invoices not disputed within twenty (20) days are deemed accepted.

## 3. Service Levels

Provider will maintain 99.5% monthly uptime for all service tiers, measured over
a calendar month excluding scheduled maintenance windows.

## 4. Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on ninety (90) days
written notice. Fees paid in advance are refunded pro rata.
