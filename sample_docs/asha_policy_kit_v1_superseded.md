## 2. Premium and Payment

The renewal premium is due within thirty (30) days of the renewal notice.
Premiums are exclusive of applicable taxes.

## 3. Cover and Benefits

A compulsory excess of ₹1,000 applies to each own damage claim. The Insured's
Declared Value is ₹7,10,000 as recorded in the Schedule. Windscreen replacement
is covered up to one claim per period of insurance.
